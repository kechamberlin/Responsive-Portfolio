About Me

My name is Kris Chamberlin. I am 27-years-old and live in Irvine, California. In June 2019, I graduated magna cum laude from the University of California, Santa Barbara with a B. A. in History. Currently, I am at UC Irvine doing a Full Stack Web Development boot camp. I want to be a web developer because coding is a very in-demand skill employers look for in today's economy. Prior to taking the boot camp, I had zero experience with coding. However, I have learned a lot within the first week!

I live with my wonderful girlfriend Aleah, who I have been with for about a year and a half, and my number one favorite thing to do in the world is make fun memories with her.

In my spare time, I enjoy playing video games. Currently, my favorite games are World of Warcraft: Classic (a high-fantasy MMORPG) and Hearts of Iron IV (a grand strategy World War II simulator). Additionally, I aspire to participate in a powerlifting competition, poorly play the electric bass, and attend concerts of my favorite bands when my bank account allows me to. My favorite music genre is heavy metal, and I am absolutely obsessed with Iron Maiden and Metallica.
